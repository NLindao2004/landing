One Piece

One Piece tells the story of a young man named Monkey D. Luffy, who, inspired by his pirate friend Shanks, begins a journey to achieve his dream of becoming the Pirate King. To do so, he must find the One Piece treasure left by the previous Pirate King, Gol D. Roger.

Mugiwuaras

The Straw Hat Pirates (麦わら海賊団 Mugiwara Kaizokudan), sometimes referred to as the Straw Hat Crew (麦わらの一味 Mugiwara no Ichimi), are the main protagonists of the One Piece manga series written and drawn by Eiichirō Oda, and main characters in its spin-off media franchise.

Devil fruits

Mystical fruits that allow you to have different types of abilities. There are three different types. There are the paramecia, the most common, the zoan, which turn the person who consumes them into an animal, and the logia, the strangest and those that contain a great power of destruction and control.

Gears Luffy

Throughout his adventure, Luffy has found new ways to use his Devil Fruit in combat, creating different forms that he calls Gears. In Wano, his fruit awakened after his apparent "death" and fourth defeat against Kaidou, strengthening his rubbery body and granting him more freedom.
